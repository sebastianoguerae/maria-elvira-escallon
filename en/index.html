Encounters in the Dust Factory

A retrospective look at three decades of work interrogating the boundaries between nature and culture, memory and history, decay and permanence. The exhibition brings together for the first time the broadest collection of Escallón's work in a single space.

About the artist

Colección Seguros Bolívar de Arte Contemporáneo, 2018 Her work articulates sculptural processes, photography and in-situ work, revealing a present threatened by historical failures, positioning itself between history and memory by establishing thresholds to connect times and fragments of stories through elements of material culture.

Biography

María Elvira Escallón does not illustrate violence. She points. She does not criticize — she evidences.

Born in London in 1954, Escallón has developed over three decades a body of work that interrogates the boundaries between the natural and the constructed, memory and oblivion, decay and permanence.
