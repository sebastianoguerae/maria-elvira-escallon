Ensayo autorretrato

2022  /  Video  /  Galeria Espacio Continuo, Bogota

In video, the artist's hands search for something on a wall, scratching with their nails until they find a nail. In another sequence, her hands systematically destroy a Venus de Milo mask with various mallets, and then model an arm that connects not to a shoulder but to a frailejon — a plant of the Colombian paramos.

The self-portrait is constructed not from the face but from the hands: hands that extract, that destroy canons of beauty, that model the connection between the human and the vegetal. The work uses molds that seek to recreate the artist's face, which are grated with a cheese grater in a gesture that undoes conventional representation.

CONTEXT

Ensayo autorretrato is part of the exhibition "Encuentros con seres notables" at Galeria Espacio Continuo (2022). The video works reveal Escallon's process — the hands as sculptural agents, destroying and creating simultaneously. The self-portrait is defined not by the representation of the face but by the action of the hands: artistic identity as gesture, as process, as relationship with matter.
