VIDEO

Extraccion

Extraccion is a single-channel video that records a delicate and repetitive gesture: a hand carefully extracts small fragments of matter from the skin. The action, filmed through a circular lens reminiscent of a medical or scientific instrument, transforms an everyday act into an almost surgical observation of the relationship between the body and its material residues.
